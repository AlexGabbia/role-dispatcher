# Management & Organization / Gestione e Organizzazione

| # | Role | Description | Reports To |
|---|------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | CTO (Chief Technology Officer) | Executive responsible for the company's technology strategy | CEO / Board |
| 2 | CIO (Chief Information Officer) | Executive responsible for information systems and digital transformation | CEO / Board |
| 3 | VP of Engineering | Leads the entire software engineering organization | CTO |
| 4 | IT Director | Directs the IT department and defines technology priorities | CIO / CTO |
| 5 | CDO (Chief Data Officer) | Executive responsible for enterprise data strategy and governance | CEO / Board |
| 6 | Product Manager | Defines product vision and guides development based on user needs | VP of Engineering / CTO |
| 7 | Engineering Manager | Manages the development team, combining technical leadership and people management | VP of Engineering |
| 8 | Project Manager (PM) | Plans, coordinates and oversees IT projects within time and budget | IT Director / Delivery Manager |
| 9 | Program Manager | Coordinates multiple related projects to achieve strategic objectives | IT Director / VP of Engineering |
| 10 | Tech Lead | Guides team technical decisions and serves as architecture reference | Engineering Manager |
| 11 | Product Owner | Manages the product backlog and priorities within the Agile framework | Product Manager |
| 12 | Business Analyst | Analyzes business processes and translates requirements into technical specifications | Project Manager / Product Manager |
| 13 | Delivery Manager | Ensures projects are delivered with quality and on schedule | IT Director / VP of Engineering |
| 14 | Technical Program Manager | Manages complex programs with strong technical components and cross-team dependencies | VP of Engineering / CTO |
| 15 | Scrum Master | Facilitates the Scrum process, removing obstacles and promoting Agile practices | Engineering Manager / Delivery Manager |
| 16 | Agile Coach | Guides the organization in adopting and improving Agile methodologies | VP of Engineering / CTO |
| 17 | Change Manager | Manages organizational change related to new technology adoption | CIO / IT Director |
