# Consulting & Training / Consulenza e Formazione

| # | Role | Description | Reports To |
|---|------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | Enterprise Architect | Defines the overall IT architecture of an organization, aligning it with business strategy | CTO / CIO |
| 2 | Solution Architect | Designs complex technical solutions that meet business requirements | Enterprise Architect / CTO |
| 3 | Cloud Architect | Designs scalable, secure and cost-optimized cloud architectures | Enterprise Architect / CTO |
| 4 | Software Architect | Defines high-level architecture of software applications | Enterprise Architect / VP of Engineering |
| 5 | IT Consultant | Provides strategic and technical consulting to companies on IT technologies and processes | CTO / IT Director |
| 6 | Pre-Sales Engineer | Supports the sales team with technical demonstrations and proof of concepts | Sales Operations Manager / CTO |
| 7 | Technical Account Manager | Manages technical relationships with clients, ensuring satisfaction and adoption | Customer Success Manager / Sales Operations Manager |
| 8 | Technical Writer | Writes technical documentation: manuals, guides, API references | Engineering Manager / Product Manager |
| 9 | Technical Trainer | Trains employees and clients on software, tools and technologies | IT Director / Engineering Manager |
| 10 | Technology Evangelist | Promotes technologies and platforms through presentations, articles and communities | CTO / CMO Digital |
| 11 | IT Researcher | Conducts research on emerging technologies to evaluate enterprise adoption | CTO / Enterprise Architect |
| 12 | Academic/University Researcher | Conducts academic research in computer science at universities and research centers | Department Director |
| 13 | IT Mentor/Coach | Mentors IT professionals in their technical and career growth | Engineering Manager / IT Director |
