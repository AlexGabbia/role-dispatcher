# E-commerce & Marketplace / E-commerce e Marketplace

| # | Role | Description | Reports To |
|---|------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | Marketplace Manager | Manages company presence on marketplaces like Amazon, eBay and Etsy | E-commerce Director |
| 2 | E-commerce Director | Directs the overall e-commerce strategy of the company | CMO Digital / CEO |
| 3 | Marketplace Account Manager | Manages seller accounts on marketplaces, optimizing sales and visibility | Marketplace Manager |
| 4 | E-commerce Product Manager | Manages the online product catalog and coordinates its evolution | E-commerce Director |
| 5 | Catalog Manager | Curates product listings, pricing and categories on sales platforms | E-commerce Product Manager / E-commerce Director |
| 6 | Marketplace Operations Specialist | Manages logistics, orders and operational performance on marketplaces | Marketplace Manager |
| 7 | E-commerce Analyst | Analyzes sales, conversions and e-commerce KPIs to guide decisions | E-commerce Director / E-commerce Product Manager |
| 8 | Dropshipping Manager | Manages the dropshipping model (selling without own inventory) | E-commerce Director / Marketplace Manager |
| 9 | Marketplace Advertising Specialist | Manages advertising campaigns within marketplaces (Amazon Ads, etc.) | Marketplace Manager / Performance Marketing Manager |
| 10 | Pricing Analyst / Dynamic Pricing Specialist | Optimizes prices in real-time with algorithms and market analysis | E-commerce Director / E-commerce Product Manager |
